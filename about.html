In the year 1920, in the heart of Satara, Maharashtra, Shri. B.M. Latkar started a small sweet shop with nothing but a passion for traditional Indian mithai and a commitment to quality. What began as a modest storefront has now blossomed into a beloved institution, carrying forward three generations of sweet-making expertise.

For over 100 years, Latkar Sweets has been synonymous with authentic Maharashtrian sweets, particularly our legendary Kandi Pedha - a recipe perfected over decades and passed down through generations.
