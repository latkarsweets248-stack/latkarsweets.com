Satari Kandi Pedha

Ingredients

Pure buffalo milk khoya,pollish sugar, cardamom powder.

Speciality

Satara’s most iconic mithai known for its rich texture, heavenly aroma, and soft melt-in-mouth feel.

Origin

Kandi Pedha originates from Satara (Maharashtra), famous across India for its unique slow-cooked milk preparation.

History Behind the Name

The pedha was historically made in small “kands” (batches) over wooden fire. Locals started calling it “Kandi Cha Pedha” which later became Kandi Pedha.

What Makes Latkar Sweets Special?

We follow the 100-year-old slow-cooking tradition, using brass vessels (patila), natural ingredients,and traditional way of using coal bhatti and zero preservatives.

Our Thoughts

This pedha is not just a sweet—it's a memory of Maharashtra’s heritage. Every bite tells a story of tradition, patience, and purity.
