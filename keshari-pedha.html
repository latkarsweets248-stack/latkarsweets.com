Keshari Pedha

Ingredients

Pure buffalo milk khoya, sugar and keshar.

Speciality

Soft, aromatic, and saffron-flavored pedha with a bright golden hue.

Origin

Maharashtra, India – a specialty sweet made in Satara.

History Behind the Name

The pedha is called Keshari because of the generous use of kesar (saffron) giving it a golden color and unique aroma.

What Makes Latkar Sweets Special?

Slow-cooked khoya with natural saffron for authentic taste on traditional coal bhatti.

Our Thoughts

A royal treat that melts in your mouth, perfect for festivals and celebrations.
