Malai Barfi

Ingredients

Fresh malai,pure buffalo milk, pollish sugar.

Speciality

Creamy, soft, and lightly sweet barfi with rich malai flavor.

Origin

India, commonly made in Maharashtra and across North India for festivals.

History Behind the Name

“Malai” means cream, which gives this barfi its smooth, rich texture.

What Makes Latkar Sweets Special?

We use fresh malai and slow-cook to perfection for soft, melt-in-mouth sweetness.

Our Thoughts

Delicious, creamy, and perfect for gifting or celebrations.
